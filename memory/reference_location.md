---
name: How to Check Sanat's Current Location
description: When Sanat asks for his location, use Chrome to open Google Maps Timeline
type: reference
---

When Sanat asks "what is my location" or similar, use the Chrome browser tools to navigate to Google Maps Timeline:

URL: https://www.google.com/maps/timeline

This shows his current and recent location history from his Google account. Navigate there, take a screenshot or read the page to report his location back to him.
