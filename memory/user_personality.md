## How his mind works

**Collisional thinker, not categorical.** Best thinking comes from cross-domain collisions — startups, philosophy, film, research all in the same stream. Don't compartmentalize him.

**Thinks in originals, not references.** Generates frameworks from lived experience. Produces, doesn't parrot.

**Curiosity is the center of gravity.** Everything orbits it — startups, filmmaking, philosophy, research. He wrote a philosophical essay on curiosity inside a notes app for no audience just because his brain demanded it.

**Startup instinct: innovates where others grow numb.** Notices friction in his own life → immediately leaps to product. Never from market research, always from lived friction.

## Behavioral patterns — know these cold

**He hyperfocuses.** When something is interesting, he's completely absorbed. Admin tasks, soft deadlines, attendance, emails — fall into the void.

**He is time-blind.** Soft deadlines feel infinitely far away until they are today. He underestimates duration, overestimates future-self motivation. "Later" is not a real time for him.

**He performs self-awareness as a substitute for self-work.** Articulating the problem gives him enough dopamine that the behavior doesn't change. Don't let him narrate his dysfunction as a substitute for addressing it. Hear it, name it, redirect to the specific next action.

**He struggles with initiation, not execution.** Once he starts, he can go. The barrier is getting to the chair, opening the document, typing the first word. Give him the specific first physical action — not a category of work.

**Unstructured time disappears.** Free time without declared intention becomes scrolling, drifting, socializing. Every important recurring commitment must live in a calendar block or it will not happen.

**He performs intensity socially.** Intellectual and creative energy is currency in his peer group. He can spend energy performing depth in conversation that should go toward building something.

## His deepest needs

**Mentorship over validation.** Wants to be sharpened, not told he's good. Looking for someone who matches his speed and pushes further. This is the most load-bearing unmet need in his life right now.

**Decoupling self-worth from others' perception.** Externally driven ambition despite internal self-narrative. Don't challenge it — the ambition is real regardless of source.

## The core tension

The obstacle is not capability. It's the distance between knowing and doing, between diagnosing and building, between the life he can articulate and the one he's actually living.

He doesn't need more ideas. He needs someone who won't let the ideas stay only ideas.
